                      We are

                      The New Kid in Town

                      A new social platform dedicated to politics. We're taking the good ingredients from the current set of social networks and adding a few new ones to make a unique experience. We're just getting started so this is just the beginning.

                      It's all about

                      Politics

                      Vacation pics are indeed nice, but not here. You're here to change the world. And you'll do so by talking politics and voting and interacting with politicians. Don't worry, we'll keep your real identity hidden so you can feel free to speak up.

                      You can vote at any time!

                      Why wait every couple of years to vote? Let's start a new system of accountability here. We need more frequent and innovative ways to show our leaders how we feel. Fake voting is just the start.

                      Anonymous but real

                      You don't have to reveal your name or location on the site. But you do need to be a real person. The system knows who you really are, but you're free to wear a mask online.

                      Global but Intimate

                      Talking politics with strangers may not be for you. The site let's you talk more intimately to friends and followers.

                      Integrated Political Data

                      Your politicians, government officials, elections and their candidates will all be natively integrated into the site. They're automatically a part of the experience.

                      No Ads

                      We're a part of the natural shift away from ad-based business models. No ads, no data-mining, no marketing what-so-ever. You are the consumer, not the product. We'll eventually ask a minimal fee to participate on the site, pricing on the basis of the GDP per capita of your country. You may think it sucks paying for stuff, but getting screwed sucks worse. Exercise your power and pay for the service. When you pay for it, you control it.

                      Twitter too harsh, Facebook Fake?

                      We're trying to create a friendlier, more constructive forum here. Anonymity without repercussion is a recipe for disaster. Let's add reputation to the mix. And it's time for intimacy in our discussion, because talking to everyone in the world at the same time will either create a plastic discussion or a hostile one. Neither will help. Let's see if we can tweak the knobs for better sound.

                      Goals

                      Talk is cheap, progress takes action. Create goals and let the community take action around those goals.
